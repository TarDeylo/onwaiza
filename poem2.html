My Muse, My Midnight Flame


Beneath the moon’s shy, silver gaze,
I find myself lost in your dazzling maze,
Eyes that dance like midnight stars,
Pulling me in, erasing scars.
Your laughter's a song that sets me free,
Soft as silk, wild as the sea.
With every glance, you melt the night,
A fire igniting pure delight.
The way you linger in my thoughts,
Drives me wild, and oh, I’m caught—
Each word, each smile, a sweet tease,
A spell that brings me to my knees.
You’re not just a dream or a fantasy,
You’re my electric ecstasy.
So here’s my heart—take all of me,
Because with you, love, I’m finally free.

A Poem Of You

I met you once, and life began to bloom,
A quiet light that lifted every room.
You came like dawn, soft and bright,
Turning my shadows into morning light.
Before you, days were simple and plain,
A constant drizzle, a gentle rain.
But with your laugh, your spark, your grace,
Suddenly the world felt a warmer place.
Your eyes hold stories of stars and skies,
Depths that pull, like oceans rise.
I find my peace, my hope, my home,
In the way you smile, in the love you’ve shown.
If words could paint or dreams could show,
The beauty you brought, the love I now know,
I’d write it all, but words fall shy,
Of the wonder you are, my endless sky.
